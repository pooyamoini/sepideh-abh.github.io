Internship

Currently, I'm doing my internship in Quantum Circuit Group under the supervision of Prof.Huard and Quantum Dr.Bienfait. In the past few weeks, I've learned about Josephson Junction, Transmon, Wigner Quasiprobability Distribution, Dispersive Coupling, SNR and quantum efficiency, Single-Shot readout, GRAPE algorithm, Rabi frequency, Jaynes Cummings Model, etc. It has been the most amazing academic experience of my life. There have been so many kind people with great knowledge who have helped me through my internship and thought me things that can't easily be found in textbooks.
